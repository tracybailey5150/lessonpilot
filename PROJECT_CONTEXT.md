# LessonPilot — Project Context

## What It Is
AI-powered learning platform that generates structured courses from any topic, with lessons, quizzes, RAG-based Q&A, progress tracking, TTS narration, and course sharing.

## Who It Serves
Students, self-learners, certification exam preppers, educators, and bootcamp participants who want AI-generated structured learning paths.

## Problem It Solves
Creating structured course content is time-consuming. LessonPilot auto-generates lessons, quizzes, study guides, and weakness-targeted review from any topic or uploaded document, with RAG-powered Q&A for deeper understanding.

## Current Stage
- Core platform live with course creation, AI lesson generation, quiz system, RAG Q&A, progress tracking.
- Stripe billing integrated (checkout + portal).
- Course sharing and resource management functional.
- TTS narration for audio learning.
